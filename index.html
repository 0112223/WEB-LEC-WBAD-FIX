About Us

Discover our book store story

Welcome to Bookly – Your Gateway to the World of Books Bookly is more than just an online bookstore. It is a platform built with passion, creativity, and a shared love for reading by five dedicated students from Bina Nusantara (Binus) University. As part of our academic journey and entrepreneurial spirit, we created Bookly to provide a seamless and enjoyable book shopping experience for everyone. We believe that books have the power to inspire, educate, and transform lives. That’s why our mission is to make books more accessible and enjoyable for readers of all ages and interests. At Bookly, you’ll find a curated collection of fiction, non-fiction, academic resources, and more – all carefully selected to spark curiosity and fuel knowledge. Thank you for supporting our journey. We hope Bookly can become your favorite place to discover your next great read.
